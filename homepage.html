About TLIMS

Revolutionizing rental management for the JHCSC community

Our Purpose

The Tenant-Landlord Information and Management System (TLIMS) is a web-based platform designed for the JH Cerilles State College - Pagadian Campus (JHCSC) community. Our mission is to bridge the gap between students seeking accommodation and landlords offering rental properties near the campus.

We understand the challenges students face when searching for safe, affordable housing, and the difficulties landlords encounter in managing their properties.

Who We Serve

  • Students: JHCSC students seeking quality boarding houses near campus
  • Landlords: Property owners renting to responsible tenants

Our Scope

  • Property listing and management
  • Interactive map integration
  • Real-time notifications and communication
  • Secure authentication and role-based access

Our Mission

To provide a safe, efficient, and user-friendly platform that simplifies the rental process, promotes transparent communication, and builds trust between students and landlords.

Our Vision

To become the premier digital solution for student housing management, creating a thriving ecosystem where students can find their ideal home and landlords can efficiently manage their properties.
